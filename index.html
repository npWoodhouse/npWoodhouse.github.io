About Me

Hello visitor, My name is Nolan Woodhouse, and I am a student at Boise State University where I study in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile (GIMM) and Geosciences (Rocks). Currently I expect to graduate in spring of 2027. I specialize in 2D pixel art / animation for characters, user interface, and environments. I am most familiar with the Unity engine, but I am learning Unreal and Godot.

This is my portfolio website where I show off some of the cool things I’ve made. So, enjoy looking at my art and playing some of the games I’ve worked on.
